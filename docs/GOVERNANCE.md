# Governance Document for emberpost

## Mission and Goals

emberpost standardizes goalie or concierge slack posts and simplifies the setup

## Roles and Responsibilities

### Maintainers
- **List of Maintainers**: JSChronicles
- **Responsibilities**:
  - Review and merge pull requests.
  - Manage issues and discussions.
  - Ensure the project adheres to its goals and guidelines.

### Contributors
- **How to Contribute**: Review our [Contributing Guidelines](https://github.com/JSChronicles/emberpost/blob/main/docs/CONTRIBUTING.md)
- **Expectations**:
  - Follow the code of conduct.
  - Adhere to coding standards and guidelines.

## Communication Channels: Review our [Support Info](https://github.com/JSChronicles/emberpost/blob/main/docs/SUPPORT.md)
- **GitHub Issues**: For bug reports and feature requests.

## Decision-Making Process

Decisions are made through open discussion on GitHub issues and pull requests. For significant changes, a formal vote may be required among maintainers.
